"​#1 Haunted Attraction in the U.S."

Terror Behind the Walls consists of six haunted attractions that create a seamless experience. All six attractions are included in one admission price.

Attractions

Lock Down

You enter the looming façade of Cellblock 12, said to be the most haunted location inside the penitentiary. Rioting zombie have taken over, zombie guards have lost control, and the chaotic scene is filled with thick fog and mangled bars.

Machine Shop

Hidden deep inside the cellblocks is a long-forgotten machine shop. Evil pervades this space – an evil with one mind but with many bodies. Will you survive, or will you become just another cog in the machine?

Infirmary

Infirmary takes the fear of hospitals to a whole new level. In this newly revamped and highly interactive attraction, you'll encounter maniacal surgeons, orderlies, dentists, and nurses who are sharpening their scalpels for some unusual, unnatural operations.

Blood Yard

Deep within the ruins of the prison compound, you discover the camp of a gruesome colony. A path marked with bones leads you deeper into the lair of the butcher, the warriors, and their ancient empress - the forgotten who now reside among the fragments of the lost prison.

Quarantine 4D

Inside, you learn of an infectious outbreak, whose symptoms include hallucinations, blurry vision, distorted depth perception, and other mind-altering effects. Flat walls appear to have depth, and some brave visitors will be challenged to face their WORST FEARS.

Break Out

Just when you think you've found freedom, you find yourself in the middle of a prison break! Zombies surround you using every way imaginable to escape, including barrels, ropes, tunnels, ladders and crates.

Remix

With the light from just a single glowstick leading the way, you will be left to find your own way out of the pitch-black, narrow back hallways of the 10-acre penitentiary.
